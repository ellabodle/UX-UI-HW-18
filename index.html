    Tagline

    I love creating solutions to problems that are fun, appealing, and bring joy into everyday life. I have a background in mass-market wholesale apparel and getting product in the hands of the masses. I constantly think about the end user and how they experience the final product. If they don't like it, there’s no return customer. Taking that experience into UX, helped me become obsessed with creating beautiful, usable, and useful digital products that help optimize happiness. I’m not afraid to change things up. I’m certianly not afraid to be colorful. I’m not afraid to fail. A little retro, a little futuristic.
